01Executive Summary & Provider Profile

Oakwell Business Park operates a 12,000 sq ft co-working facility across three floors at Oakwell Business Park, Shirley, Solihull B90 8AG, Birmingham. This document sets out the cleaning specification, service frequencies, quality standards, and commercial terms proposed by Sparkshire Facilities Ltd for a 12-month managed cleaning contract.

The quality of a co-working environment is a direct competitive factor. Members choose and renew based on the cleanliness, freshness, and presentation of the space. Sparkshire Facilities Ltd has been delivering commercial cleaning to professional and co-working facilities since 2011. Every operative is directly employed, DBS checked, and BICSc trained. We do not use agency staff.

06Contract Terms & Acceptance

Term: 12 months from commencement date. Auto-renews annually. 60 days' written notice to terminate at renewal.

Payment: Monthly in advance, net 30 days. Late payment interest at 8% above Bank of England base rate.

Insurance: Sparkshire Facilities Ltd maintains public liability insurance of £5,000,000 and employer's liability insurance of £10,000,000. Certificates available on request.

Liability: Capped at 12 months' fees. No indirect or consequential losses. Nothing limits liability for death, personal injury, or fraud.

Confidentiality: Mutual. Survives termination by 3 years.

Governing Law: England and Wales.
