04 — Proposed Service

What's included

▶ Managed Helpdesk — Unlimited Support

Unlimited support for all 40 users. Mon–Fri 08:00–18:00 + P1 out-of-hours. Named engineers who know your environment. P1 response: 15 minutes (SLA). P2: 2 hours. P3: 4 business hours.

▶ Proactive Infrastructure Monitoring

24/7 monitoring of all servers, workstations, and network devices. Automated alerting and remediation. Monthly infrastructure health report to Helen Ashworth, Managing Partner.

▶ Microsoft 365 Management

Full M365 administration: user lifecycle, Exchange Online, SharePoint, Teams, Intune, Defender for Business. Quarterly Secure Score review and remediation roadmap.

▶ Cyber Security Programme

Managed EDR, email security (anti-phishing, DMARC), DNS filtering, privileged access management, staff security awareness training. 12-month Cyber Essentials Plus certification pathway.

▶ Business Continuity & Cloud Backup

Documented BCP/DR plan, tested annually. Cloud backup with 30-day retention, 4-hour RTO. Monthly backup verification reports.

▶ Virtual CTO — Quarterly Reviews

Quarterly strategic IT review. Technology roadmap, budget planning, compliance advisory. Written report within 5 business days.
