One-off fees are invoiced on project commencement with payment due within 14 days. Monthly services are invoiced on the 1st of each month by direct debit or BACS transfer.

Service Level Agreement

Service levels are as defined in the SLA schedule attached to the contract. Credits apply for any breach of agreed response or resolution times.

Our total liability is limited to the fees paid in the preceding 12 months. We carry £10m public liability and professional indemnity insurance.
